About Metal Music

From its rebellious roots to its thunderous evolution.

The Origins

Metal emerged in the late 1960s and early 70s with bands like Black Sabbath, Led Zeppelin, and Deep Purple. Known for its powerful guitar riffs, intense drumming, and often dark themes, metal quickly carved out a rebellious space in music history.

Major Subgenres

Thrash Metal

Fast tempos and aggressive riffs. Popularized by bands like Slayer and Megadeth.

Black Metal

Dark, atmospheric, and often lo-fi. Originated in Scandinavia with bands like Mayhem and Burzum.

Power Metal

Melodic and epic with fantasy themes. Think of bands like Blind Guardian and Helloween.
